WELCOME TO GHOST!

GHOST is a minimalist squash movement visualizer designed to turn raw athletic effort into cinematic data art. Unlike traditional video analysis that focuses on the player's physique, GHOST strips away the noise, leaving behind only the "ghost" of the movement.

By utilizing computer vision and perspective-correcting homography, GHOST maps real-world court positions onto a digital floor, allowing players to see their movement on court.

While GHOST is an animation tool, its utility lies in its ability to generate "clean" data. It turns a messy video into a structured data asset that can be used by players, squash coaches, or even data scientists to understand how players move on court.

HOW TO USE

SET UP your phone in a stable position that has the whole court in frame.

COURT CALIBRATION: Before the AI can track you, it needs to understand the "plane" of the floor. You must define the boundaries by tapping the four corners of the court on your screen in this specific order:

  1. Front-Left (where the side wall meets the tin)
  2. Front-Right
  3. Back-Right (near the back glass)
  4. Back-Left

    5. TIP: The red "ghost box" will appear on your screen once all four points are set. If the box looks skewed or doesn't align with the floorboards, hit RESET and try again for better accuracy.

RECORDING: Once calibrated, the AI locks onto your ankles or hips. Hit START RECORDING and begin your rally or ghosting drill.

The system tracks your X/Y coordinates in real-time. When finished, hit STOP & SAVE and name your session.

ANALYZE & EXPORT: Visit PAST RALLIES to review your movement patterns. Toggle the GHOST TRAIL to see your recovery speed and T-occupancy.

Use EXPORT VIDEO to save a clean, background-free animation of your movement to share with coaches or on social media.
